Who we are

TAMOHA is one of the registered Non Governmental Organization under the name and style of TAMOHA functioning in various parts of India. TAMOHA is a National Character Registered Society from Govt. of NCT of Delhi under Society registration Act XXI of 1860 and FCR Act from Govt. of India and 12 A & 80G under Section of the Income tax Act 1961.This Foundation has been started with a view to deliver superior community development programs and projects to underprivileged. "TAMOHA is one of the leading humanitarian organizations fighting for the action oriented improvemization of quality standards of our nation, by implementing different programs with newer techniques and realistic approach."
